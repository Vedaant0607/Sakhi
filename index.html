SAKHI

An Initiative By The Ladies Of Novus Society.

We aim to bring harmony amongst all our sisters through interaction,
meetings,society development and charitable works whick would not
only enhance our inner selves but also the environment around us.

This is all aimed as a Total Welfare Activity,wherein all owner and tenant
ladies automatically become members and no registration or monthly fee
collection of any kind would be made. The club works on sponsorship
format.The club will meet monthly two times, once for a beneficial cause
and second to add value through entertainment for all its members.
